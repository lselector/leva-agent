__Yes, LinkedIn is covered__ — but through the __CDP (Chrome DevTools Protocol)__ approach in Part 2 of the plan, not through an official API.

__Why CDP for LinkedIn (not an official API):__ LinkedIn's official API is extremely restricted — they don't allow personal feed reading or liking posts via API for individual developers. So browser automation is the only practical way.

The CDP approach is much better than our current Playwright setup because:

- __Connects to your already-running Chrome__ — no need to close Chrome first
- __Uses your existing LinkedIn login session__ — no profile conflicts
- __Faster__ — no browser launch overhead, just connects to what's already open
- __Less detectable__ — looks like normal browsing to LinkedIn

__What you'll be able to do:__

- See your LinkedIn feed posts (who posted what)
- Like posts from your connections
